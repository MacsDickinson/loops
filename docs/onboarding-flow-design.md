## Executive Summary

This document specifies the complete onboarding experience for Discovery Loop Coach beta users. The design prioritizes **time to value**: getting users from signup to their first complete spec in under 15 minutes.

**Key Design Principle:** Progressive disclosure with mudita (warm encouragement). We celebrate progress, guide without gatekeeping, and make the first successful outcome feel achievable.

**Success Criteria (from PRD):**
- 70% of signups complete first spec within 24 hours
- Users create 3+ specs in first week
- Average time to first spec: <15 minutes
