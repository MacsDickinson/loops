## Content Theme Reference

- **Theme 1**: The Bottleneck Migration - As AI compresses build time, bottlenecks migrate upstream (discovery) and downstream (operationalisation)
- **Theme 2**: Feedback Loops as Universal Language - Agile, DevOps, Lean all work by tightening feedback loops; AI is another loop-tightening technology
- **Theme 3**: The Discovery Gap - Requirements clarity problem becomes critical when build is fast
- **Theme 4**: Production as the New Frontier - Cheap prototypes create expensive production expectations
- **Theme 5**: Role Evolution, Not Role Replacement - Jobs evolve as build gets faster

## Posting Cadence Rules

### LinkedIn
- **Frequency**: 2-3x per week
- **Ideal days**: Monday (start the week conversation) and Thursday (mid-week engagement)
- **Timing**: 7-9am PT for best reach
- **Format**: 150-300 words, punchy opening, data/question, clear POV

### Substack
- **Frequency**: 1x per month (typically Week 2 or Week 6 of each month)
- **Ideal day**: Friday (weekend reading)
- **Format**: 1500-2500 word deep dive
- **Distribution**: Tease on LinkedIn Monday after publication

### Engagement Days
- **Week 4, 8, 12**: Dedicated community engagement, no new posts
- **Focus**: HN, Reddit (r/ExperiencedDevs, r/ExperiencedPMs), LeadDev Slack, respond to all comments
